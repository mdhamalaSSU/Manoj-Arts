About Manoj,

Manoj is a mixed media artist, designer, illustrator and creator, living and working in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Originally from Kathmandu, Nepal, his work is based on intuition, personal experiences, exploring the nature of reality by the means of various forms of illustrations and paintings including graffitis, charcoal sketch, acrylic painting and portraiture.

Through his artwork, he expresses his interpretation of nature, celebrities, human emotions and modern lifestyles like fashion designing and sneaker paintings. Each piece takes us on a unique and personal journey, inspired to cultivate curiosity, pay attention to dreams, and appreciate a magical world within and around us.

He enjoys coming up with innovative, new, and refreshing ideas and executing them in new approaches. For the past 5 years   he has been doing lot of remarkable illustrations, wall paintings, sketches and designs.
